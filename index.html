Welcome to LARQ – Hydration, Evolved

At LARQ, we believe hydration is more than just a necessity. It’s an integral part of living a healthy, balanced lifestyle. That’s why we’ve reinvented the water bottle—combining innovation, sustainability, and wellness in one sleek design. Whether you're at home, at the gym, or on an adventure, LARQ’s self-cleaning water bottles and filtration systems ensure you always have access to fresh, pure water without the need for single-use plastic.

Our Story

LARQ was born from a simple yet powerful idea: create a self-cleaning bottle that helps reduce our carbon footprint while providing access to clean drinking water. Our mission is to help people everywhere make the switch to more sustainable hydration by offering an alternative to plastic water bottles.

What started as a small project has since grown into a global movement. LARQ combines cutting-edge technology with thoughtful design to create products that are not only good for you but good for the planet too. Our bottles utilize patented PureVis™ UV-C LED technology, which neutralizes up to 99.9999% of harmful bacteria and viruses, ensuring that every sip is pure, clean, and safe.

Our vision extends far beyond just creating a bottle. We're creating a lifestyle rooted in sustainability, health, and mindful consumption. By replacing disposable plastic bottles with a sustainable, reusable solution, we’re helping you do your part in reducing plastic waste and preserving our planet for future generations.
