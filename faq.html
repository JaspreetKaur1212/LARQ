While the LARQ bottle's self-cleaning feature is excellent for maintaining cleanliness, there are a few tips to ensure it remains in top condition. First, regularly activate the self-cleaning cycle to eliminate bacteria and odor. Second, wash the cap and bottle by hand with warm water and a mild soap solution. Do not submerge the cap in water. Finally, ensure the bottle is dried thoroughly after washing to avoid any build-up of moisture.

UV-C light is a powerful ultraviolet radiation that kills bacteria, viruses, and other harmful microorganisms by disrupting their DNA. LARQ uses a specific wavelength of UV-C light (around 260 to 280 nanometers) that is particularly effective in sterilizing water. The self-cleaning mechanism in the LARQ bottle utilizes this UV-C light to purify the water inside, ensuring that it is free from contaminants that may cause illness or unpleasant odors.

The LARQ bottle is designed for water and water-based beverages.
